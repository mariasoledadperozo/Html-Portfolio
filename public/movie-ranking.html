Best Movies Of All Time by Sole

My top three movies of all time

01. Little Women (2019) by Greta Gerwing

I watched for the 2019 oscars, and It became my favourite movie
because Greta Gerwing turned to be my favourite director

02. 13 Going on 30 (2005) by Gary Winick

A classic for romcoms. I know every line of it, even the thriller
choreo. It can be sad, cute and funny, but it has a wounderful message
that every kid, adult or teenager should hear

03. Brokeback Mountain (2005) by Ang Lee

This one should've won the Oscars. Everyone makes jokes about
this movie, but this not the best gay movie, this is the best drama
romance movie of all time. Never cried so hard before.
PD: I miss you, Heath Ledger
